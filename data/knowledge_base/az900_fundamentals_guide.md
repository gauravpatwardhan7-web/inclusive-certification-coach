## Fundamentals track

- Primary certification: AZ-900 (Azure Fundamentals)
- Intended audience: any role new to cloud — sales, support, project managers, junior engineers.

### AZ-900 skill areas and recommended study hours

| Skill area | Weight | Recommended hours |
|---|---|---|
| Describe cloud concepts (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS, shared responsibility) | 25% | 4 |
| Describe Azure architecture and services (regions, compute, storage, networking) | 35% | 6 |
| Describe Azure management and governance (cost, Policy, Advisor, lifecycle) | 30% | 5 |
| Describe identity, access, and security basics (Entra ID, RBAC, Zero Trust) | 10% | 2 |

Total recommended study: ~17 hours.

### Recommended study pattern

- 30-60 minutes of focused study per session; this is an entry-level exam.
- A short recap quiz at the end of each skill area.
- Weekly assessment checkpoints.
- Target 75% practice score before attempting the exam.

### Accessibility guidance for this track

- Learners who report attention or focus difficulties benefit from shorter 25-minute study blocks with breaks, rather than long sessions.
- The architecture-and-services area has the most new vocabulary; provide a plain-language glossary before deep dives.
- All practice questions should be available in a text-only, screen-reader-friendly format; avoid diagram-dependent questions.
